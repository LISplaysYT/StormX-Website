Moderation

StormX simplifies the moderation of your Discord server, ensuring a seamless experience. With just a simple invitation to the bot, you're all set to enhance your server's management and maintain a positive environment!

With a customizable welcome message and automated greetings, integrating StormX into your community is effortless. Simply invite the bot and run /setup welcome-system, and enhance your server's warmth and engagement from the moment new members join!

With StormX, you can rest easy knowing that our bot is online around the clock, providing consistent support and engagement for your Discord server. This ensures that moderation and welcoming functionalities are always available, no matter the time of day.
